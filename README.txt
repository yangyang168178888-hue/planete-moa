THE GOOD CONVERSATION QUEEN PERSONAL WEBSITE
============================

This folder contains one consolidated English personal website with a warm, witty American queen tone.

SECTIONS

- Personal introduction
- International life story
- Professional experience and strengths
- Lifestyle and interests
- Values
- Facebook and email contact buttons

LOCAL PREVIEW

- Double-click start-preview.cmd
- Open http://localhost:4180

CONTACT SETTINGS

Open site-config.js and add the public contact details between the quotation marks:

facebookUrl: "https://www.facebook.com/your-public-profile",
email: "your-email@example.com",

PUBLICATION NOTES

- Only publish photographs that you own or are authorized to use.
- Keep all biography and career statements accurate and personally approved.
- The website avoids invented awards, financial results, and unverified performance claims.
- Vercel configuration is included in vercel.json.
